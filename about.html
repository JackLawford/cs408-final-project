HOW TO PLAY

  1. Start the game when prompted
  2. Wait for the green lights to illuminate
  3. Click on the gas pedal to race down the strip!
  4. Your score will be displayed at the end, then you can check the leaderboard to see how you hold up against other players! If you use the same username each time, your best score will be the one displayed on the leaderboard.

PRO TIP: The faster your reaction time is, the better your score will be. Try and predict the light change to get a better score, but be careful not to "jump" the light. This will disqualify your run!
